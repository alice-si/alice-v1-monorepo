ABOUT OUR STABLECOIN PARTNERSHIP
When Alice first launched, we were one of the first companies in the world to use a regulated stablecoin.
We participated in a pilot run by Tramonex Labs, within the Financial Conduct Authority's regulatory sandbox, to issue e-money (tokens that represent the value of fiat donations) on Ethereum.

We are currently integrated with MangoPay to ensure the stability of donations, and are open to experimenting with other stablecoin solutions.
